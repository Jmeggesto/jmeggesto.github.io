Metro-Annotation 1

Published by Jackie Meggesto on May 2, 2017 in metroannotations, poetry, writing, nyc and subway

There is an uptown

express

3 train to

(my bed, my home, my sanctuary;

a hot bath, a good meal, an old friend)

approaching the station;

please stand

(up, get ready -

the train's almost here -

you're almost home!)
